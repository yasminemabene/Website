This is the app I made. It is a trivia game on vines which is a 6 second video and an intregal part of Gen Z culture! Each page has a different vine and players have to fill in the blank with the correct statement. Our project is a trivia game featuring questions regarding the app Vine. Vine is a form of social media where people could upload 6 second videos and share them. Our questions are fill in the blank to popular lines from Vines. In order to get the answer correct, the player must spell the answer correctly. We included pictures for each question that correspond with the Vine that it came from. Our project ended up working but for future improvement we could include a scoreboard, music, and allow people to compete against each other. I think we could definitely sell this as a product or at least turn it into a monetized website like Kahoot. Quizzes like these are popular on sites such as Buzzfeed.

This is the interactive fiction game I made. It is a journey to survive a shipwreck. Our game starts off with two decisions that break into two main methods with several methods within them. One method ends up potentially leading to the other. We included a Boolean code so you can pick up items to help you along the way.

The default ratio of wolves to sheep allows for a stable population. If you increase the number of wolves to exceed the number of sheep, the sheep die out followed by all the wolves who have nothing to eat. If you decrease the growth rate and energy gained by grass, the sheep population will die out as well as the wolves.
